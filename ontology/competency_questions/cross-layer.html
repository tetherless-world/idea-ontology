CQ-X-TRACE-01: Which sentences give rise to this argument?

Intent

Trace a claim or argument back to the sentences from which it was extracted or grounded.

Natural Language Question

Which sentences give rise to this claim?

PREFIX idea: 
PREFIX po:   
PREFIX prov: 

SELECT DISTINCT ?arg ?para
WHERE {
  idea:work-semsur-2018 po:contains ?arg .
  ?arg prov:wasDerivedFrom ?para .

CQ-X-TRACE-03: What document sections contribute to the used artifacts?

Intent

Natural Language Question

What document sections contribute most to the final claim?

PREFIX rdfs: 
PREFIX idea: 
PREFIX po:   
PREFIX cito: 

SELECT DISTINCT ?para ?citationType
WHERE {
  idea:work-semsur-2018 po:contains ?arg .
  ?arg idea:realizes ?app .
  ?app idea:uses ?art .

  ?cite cito:hasCitingEntity ?art ;
        cito:hasCitedEntity ?para ;
        ?citationType ?para .

  ?citationType rdfs:subPropertyOf cito:cites .
